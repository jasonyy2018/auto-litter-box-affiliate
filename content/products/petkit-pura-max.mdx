# PETKIT Pura Max Review: Best Value Automatic Litter Box

The **PETKIT Pura Max** has quickly become one of the most popular automatic litter boxes on the market, and for good reason. It offers an impressive array of features at a more accessible price point than premium competitors.

## Overview

PETKIT has built a reputation for smart pet products, and the Pura Max represents their flagship automatic litter box. With its xSecure safety system and built-in deodorizer, it's designed to address the most common concerns cat owners have about automatic litter boxes.

**Our Rating: 4.6/5**

## Key Features

### xSecure 5-Point Safety System
The Pura Max uses multiple sensors:
- Infrared detection
- Weight sensors
- Anti-pinch door
- Motion detection
- Emergency stop

### N50 Odor Eliminator
Unlike other automatic litter boxes that rely solely on carbon filters, the Pura Max includes an active deodorizing spray system that neutralizes odors.

### Extra-Large Interior
The spacious cylinder design accommodates cats up to 18 lbs comfortably, making it suitable for most domestic cats.

### Smart App Control
The PETKIT app offers:
- Usage statistics
- Cleaning cycle customization
- Maintenance reminders
- Multi-device support

## Pros and Cons

### What We Love
- Excellent value for features ($549 vs $699)
- Very spacious interior
- Multiple safety sensors
- Built-in deodorizer
- Modern aesthetic design

### What Could Be Better
- App can be buggy at times
- Deodorizer refills add cost
- Slightly louder than competitors
