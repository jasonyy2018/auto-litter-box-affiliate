# Litter-Robot 4 vs PETKIT Pura Max

Choosing between the Litter-Robot 4 and PETKIT Pura Max? Both are excellent automatic litter boxes, but they cater to different priorities. Let's break down the key differences.

## Quick Summary

| Feature | Litter-Robot 4 | PETKIT Pura Max |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| Price | $699 | $549 |
| Capacity | Up to 4 cats | Up to 3 cats |
| Max Cat Weight | 25 lbs | 18 lbs |
| Noise Level | < 50 dB | < 55 dB |
| Odor Control | Carbon filter | Active deodorizer spray |
| App Rating | 4.5/5 | 4.2/5 |

## Litter-Robot 4 Pros
- Quieter operation
- Larger capacity
- Better for big cats
- More reliable app
- 90-day money-back guarantee

## PETKIT Pura Max Pros
- $150 cheaper
- Built-in deodorizer spray
- Modern design
- xSecure safety system
- Great value for features

## Our Verdict

**Choose Litter-Robot 4 if:**
- You have large cats or multiple cats
- Quiet operation is important
- You want the most reliable option
- Budget is less of a concern

**Choose PETKIT Pura Max if:**
- You want great features at a lower price
- Active odor control is a priority
- You have 1-3 average-sized cats
- You prefer modern aesthetics

Both are excellent choices - you can't go wrong with either one.
