# PETKIT Pura Max vs CatLink Scooper

Looking for a quality automatic litter box without the premium price tag? The PETKIT Pura Max and CatLink Scooper are two excellent mid-range options. Here's how they compare.

## Quick Summary

| Feature | PETKIT Pura Max | CatLink Scooper |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Price | $549 | $399 |
| Capacity | Up to 3 cats | Up to 2 cats |
| Noise Level | < 55 dB | < 52 dB |
| Built-in Deodorizer | Yes | No |
| App Quality | Good | Good |

## PETKIT Pura Max Highlights
- Built-in N50 deodorizer spray
- xSecure 5-point safety system
- Larger interior space
- More premium build quality

## CatLink Scooper Highlights
- Most affordable option
- Simple and reliable
- Compact footprint
- Good app with health tracking

## Our Verdict

**Choose PETKIT Pura Max if:**
- You can stretch the budget
- Active odor control matters
- You have 2-3 cats
- You want more premium features

**Choose CatLink Scooper if:**
- Budget is the priority
- You have 1-2 cats
- Space is limited
- You want reliable basics

The $150 difference gets you more features with the PETKIT, but the CatLink delivers excellent value at its price point.
