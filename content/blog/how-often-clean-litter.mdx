# How Often Should You Clean a Litter Box?

A clean litter box is essential for your cat's health and happiness. Here's your complete maintenance guide.

## Traditional Litter Box Schedule

### Daily
- Scoop solid waste and clumps 1-2 times
- Top off litter to maintain 2-3 inches depth
- Check for unusual smells

### Weekly
- Stir litter to prevent clumping at bottom
- Wipe down edges
- Add more litter if needed

### Monthly
- Completely empty and wash box
- Use mild soap and water
- Dry thoroughly before refilling

## Automatic Litter Box Schedule

### Every 7-10 Days
- Empty waste drawer
- Replace waste bag
- Check litter level

### Monthly
- Wipe down sensors
- Replace carbon filter
- Check waste drawer seal

### Every 3-6 Months
- Deep clean entire unit
- Replace all litter
- Check moving parts

## Signs Your Box Needs Cleaning
- Strong ammonia smell
- Cat avoiding the box
- Visible waste buildup
- Cat going outside box

## Pro Tips for Odor Control
1. Use quality clumping litter
2. Add baking soda to bottom
3. Consider automatic box
4. Ensure good ventilation
5. One box per cat plus one extra
