# CatLink Scooper Standard Pro Review: Best Budget Option

The **CatLink Scooper Standard Pro** proves that you don't need to spend $600+ to get a reliable automatic litter box. It offers solid performance and essential smart features at a more accessible price point.

## Overview

CatLink has positioned itself as the value-focused option in the automatic litter box market. The Scooper Standard Pro delivers the core functionality that matters most - reliable cleaning and health monitoring - without the premium price tag.

**Our Rating: 4.4/5**

## Key Features

### Double Odor Control
Combines a carbon filter with an enclosed design to minimize odors escaping into your home.

### Smart Weight Monitoring
Tracks your cat's weight with each visit, helping you monitor health changes over time.

### Health Data Tracking
The app records:
- Visit frequency
- Time spent in box
- Weight trends
- Cleaning cycles

### Easy-Clean Drawer
The waste drawer slides out easily for quick disposal and cleaning.
