# How to Choose the Best Automatic Litter Box

Choosing the right automatic litter box can feel overwhelming with so many options available. This guide walks you through everything you need to consider.

## Key Factors to Consider

### 1. Your Cat's Size
- Small to medium cats (up to 12 lbs): Most models work
- Large cats (12-18 lbs): Choose larger interiors
- Extra-large cats (18+ lbs): Litter-Robot 4 recommended

### 2. Number of Cats
- 1-2 cats: Most models work well
- 3-4 cats: Litter-Robot 4 or PETKIT Pura Max
- 5+ cats: Consider multiple units

### 3. Available Space
Measure your space before buying. Don't forget entry/exit clearance.

### 4. Noise Level
If placing in a bedroom, look for models under 50 dB.

### 5. App Features
Consider: usage tracking, health insights, remote control, notifications.

### 6. Budget
- Budget ($300-$450): CatLink Scooper
- Mid-range ($450-$600): PETKIT Pura Max
- Premium ($600+): Litter-Robot 4

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. Buying too small
2. Ignoring noise levels
3. Forgetting ongoing costs
4. Skipping the transition period
