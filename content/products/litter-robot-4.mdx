# Litter-Robot 4 Review: The Ultimate Automatic Litter Box?

The **Litter-Robot 4** is the latest iteration from Whisker (formerly AutoPets), and it's been making waves in the automatic litter box market. After months of testing with multiple cats, we're ready to share our comprehensive review.

## Overview

The Litter-Robot 4 represents a significant upgrade from its predecessor, the Litter-Robot 3. With its new OmniSense detection system, whisper-quiet operation, and sleek redesign, it aims to be the gold standard in self-cleaning litter boxes.

**Our Rating: 4.8/5**

## Key Features

### OmniSense Detection
The new sensor system uses a combination of laser and weight sensors to accurately detect when your cat enters and exits the unit. This prevents premature cycling and ensures safety.

### Whisper-Quiet Motor
One of the biggest improvements is the noise level. The Litter-Robot 4 operates at under 50 dB, making it suitable for bedrooms or quiet spaces.

### Smart App Integration
The Whisker app provides:
- Real-time waste drawer level
- Cat weight tracking
- Usage statistics
- Health insights
- Remote control

### Multi-Cat Support
Supports up to 4 cats (under 25 lbs each), making it ideal for multi-cat households.

## Pros and Cons

### What We Love
- Best-in-class odor control
